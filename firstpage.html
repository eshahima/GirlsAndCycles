WHY IS MENSTRUAL HEALTH SO IMPORTANT?

In a poll of 2,000 women:

37% were not able to correctly define the menstrual cycle.

38% of women wish they knew about women's reproductive systems.

Another study shows that:

8% of men believe that period attracts sharks.

These studies show how even in the 21st century, many people are not aware of menstrual cycles and menopause.

Girls and Cycles aims to bridge this gap by increasing awareness of menstrual education.
