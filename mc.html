What causes change in the Menstrual Cycle?

Ovulatory Dysfunction

Lack of ovulation or ovulation occuring at irregular times causes irregular periods.

Uterine Fibroids

Fibroids, growths of smooth muscle cells and fibrous connective tissue, cause heavy bleeding and periods tend to last longer.
